Members

Lisa

Lisa (리사) is a Thai rapper and singer under YG Entertainment. She is the maknae of the girl group BLACKPINK. She made her solo debut on September 10, 2021 with her first single album, "Lalisa".

Jennie

Jennie (제니) is a South Korean singer-songwriter and rapper under YG Entertainment. She is a member of the girl group BLACKPINK. She made her solo debut on November 12, 2018 with the single "Solo".

Jisoo

Jisoo (지수) is a South Korean singer, lyricist, and actress under YG Entertainment. She is a member of the girl group BLACKPINK. She will make her solo debut in 2023.

Rose

Rosé (로제) is a Korean-New Zealand singer and lyricist under YG Entertainment. She is the main vocalist of the girl group BLACKPINK. She made her solo debut on March 12, 2021 with her first single album, "-R-".
